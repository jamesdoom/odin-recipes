Pizza Sauce

  • 1(15oz can) Tomatoes*
  • 1 (6oz can) Tomato Paste
  • 1 TBS Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • Splash of Olive Oil

In a medium bowl, stir tomatoes and tomato paste until smooth. Add remaining
ingredients and stir well.

*Cento San Marzano Peeled Tomatoes preferred.
