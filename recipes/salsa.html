Salsa

Ingredients

  • 1(28oz can) Whole Peeled Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 2 Jalapenos (seeds removed)
  • 2/3 Cup Cilantro (leaves plucked from stems)
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • Pinch of Sugar

Steps

Cut onion into a few large pieces and pluck cilantro
to remove stems. Place tomatoes (with juice) in food
processor along with other ingredients.

Pulse blend desired consistency.
